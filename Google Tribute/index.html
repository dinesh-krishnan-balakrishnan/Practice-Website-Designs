Google

Timeline

  • 1995: The founders of Google, Larry Page and Sergey bian, met each other.
  • 1996: Page and bian worked on a search engine known as BackRub.
  • 1997: Google.com was officially registered.
  • 1998: Google was incorporated as a private company in a garage.
  • 2000: Google launched an April Fools prank called MentalPlex.
  • 2001-2003: Google Images, Google AdSense, and Google Trends were launched.
  • 2004: Google had over 800+ employees and opened up their headquarters in Mountain View, California.
  • 2005: Google Maps, Google Earth, and Youtube were released.
  • 2007: Google was ranked the #1 company to work for by Fortune.
  • 2007-2011: Google created Android, Chrome, Google Voice, Google+, and Google Drive.
  • 2013+: Google participates in many different projects, from creating faster interet to developing self-driving cars.
